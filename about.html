Cultural Tradition

At La Toscanella, we strive to stay true to the cultural traditions that shaped our family and inspired our recipes. We import certain key ingredients, such as our flour and ricotta, out of Lago Maggiore to give our customers an authentic experience of the flavors of the region. We want you to reimagine the way you think of Italian food.

From Wholesale To Opening Our First Bakery

In 2011, we were servicing more than 50 local bakeries as a wholesale enterprise. However, a little over a year later we were able to open up our first brick and mortar location in the heart of South Lake Union. We were welcomed into the community with open arms and were overjoyed to play a part in the transition from a bustling working sector to neighborhood.

New Beginnings

In June of 2017, we opened our second location in the South Park neighborhood of Seattle. Our main focus was to honor the legacy of this area. We designed the interior with an industrial influence, inspired by the blue collar people that we get to serve.
